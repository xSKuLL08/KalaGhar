We are team KalaGhar, an emerging organisation in the art industry creating an antistic environment for the emerging artworks around the Globe. We are building a community to spread the awareness about art to the unknown population of the Globe. We will further introduce features which will help us grow this community and provide a better future for the Art and the Artists.
